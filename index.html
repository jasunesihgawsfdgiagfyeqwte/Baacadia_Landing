BAACADIA

A healing adventure through sound

Playground
Scroll

"What if sound was a language?"

You are Scout, a robot awakening in an alien world.
Your only companions: mysterious sheep-like creatures called Cloudfens.
Your only tool: the ability to record and replay sounds.

Together, you'll learn to shepherd through acoustics,
heal a world scarred by ancient sonic warfare,
and discover that listening is more powerful than commanding.

Acoustic Shepherding

Guide creatures through sound

Contemplative Pacing

No combat, no timers

Moebius-Inspired

Ligne claire in 3D

In Baacadia, you are Scout — a recorder robot adrift in a world of drifting dunes and strange silences. The creatures here, Clofen, don't respond to commands. They respond to memory.

No orders. No leash. Just sound, stillness, and the slow pull of something that feels like belonging.

HOW IT WORKS

01
Record

Approach any sound source — crystal formations, ancient structures, unknown creatures — and hold still. Scout captures it. The world has been waiting to be heard.

02
Replay

Scout has no voice of its own. But it carries yours now. Play back what you've gathered — different sounds reach different things.

03
Drive

Emit the Sound of Drive and your Clofen mirror your every step. Stop, and they stop. It's not a command — it's an invitation.

04
Merge

The Sound of Merge pulls your flock close, gathering them from wherever they've wandered. They come not because they must — but because they recognize something familiar.

05
Shepherd

Your Clofen are natives. They know this land. Watch what frightens them, what draws them, what makes them curious — and you'll understand where to go next.

06
Restore

Ancient Noise Crystals still corrupt the land — dissonant remnants of a forgotten war. Find the source. Quiet it. Watch the world breathe again.
